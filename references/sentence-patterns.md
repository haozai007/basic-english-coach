# Sentence Patterns

Use patterns as reusable frames. Keep the words around the frame simple.

## Basic Frames

- I have ...
- I need ...
- I want ...
- I can ...
- I cannot ...
- I am going to ...
- I would like to ...
- It is ...
- This is ...
- There is ...
- There are ...
- Please give me ...
- Please send me ...
- Can you ...?
- Could you ...?
- Do you have ...?
- Is it ready?
- Is that right?
- I am not certain.
- I will check it.
- I will get back to you.

## Work Status

- I am working on it.
- It is almost ready.
- It is not ready yet.
- We need more time.
- There is a problem with ...
- I found the problem.
- I will make a change.
- I will send the new version.
- Please check this part.
- Please let me know your answer.

## Clarifying Meaning

- Do you mean ...?
- Can you say that in another way?
- I want to make certain.
- Let me say it again.
- This part is not clear to me.
- What is the most important thing here?

## Practice Method

For drills, give a frame and ask the user to replace one part:

Frame: I need ...

- I need more time.
- I need your answer.
- I need the new file.
- I need to check this.

Then ask the user to create 3 sentences with the same frame.
